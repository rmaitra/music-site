The Story

Raj Maitra (A.K.A. the Raj) grew up in Uptown, Minneapolis learning Sega Genesis soundtracks on the piano. He took backseat at South High school's Jazz band to real musicians and almost gave up music before a monumental transition. Among the legendary redwood forests of the Santa Cruz mountains in California, The Raj, as an aspiring geneticist at the University of California, awoke a new calling within music: hip hop. An avid beatboxer and classically trained pianist, he took to making hip hop beats with his fellow Banana Slug and rapper, Emil Carson. Together they have fused the natural hymns of the wilderness with gritty break beats from the 90s and topped it all off with clever jazz licks on a fender rhodes. Now their music vibrates between the Bay Area and Minneapolis.
