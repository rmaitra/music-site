The Story

Raj Maitra (A.K.A. the Raj) is a musician and producer from Uptown, Minneapolis. He was classically trained in piano since the age of 8. While pursuing his bachelors of science in Biomolecular Engineering at the University of California Santa Cruz, he transitioned into jazz and music production as a local pianist in the bay area. He scored several independent student films and regularly gigged with the Terminal Degree Jazz band. After receiving his master's of science in Computer Engineering from UCSC, he moved to San Francisco and began producing hip hop instrumentals. After producing two personal rap albums, he started to produce other local bay area artists. Now located in Minneapolis, Minnesota, he produces as well as works as a Software Manager for Simple But Needed, an Oakland-based software company.
